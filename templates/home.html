Open Tower de­vel­ops free, open source tech­nolo­gies in ser­vice of a more trans­par­ent, in­clu­sive and con­ve­nient acad­e­mia. We fo­cus on var­i­ous as­pects of aca­d­e­mic life, in­clud­ing pub­lish­ing, teach­ing and out­reach be­yond the aca­d­e­mic com­mu­nity.

Open Tower was co-founded by philoso­phers David Faraci and Graham Leach-Krouse.

The source code for this web­site, and for Open Tower’s sub­sidiary pro­jects, can be found on Open Tower’s GitHub or­ga­ni­za­tion site.

For ques­tions or com­ments, or to pro­pose a new pro­ject, please con­tact us at admin@open-tower.com.
